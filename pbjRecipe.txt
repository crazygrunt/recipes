Peanut Butter & Jelly Recipe

2: Slices of Bread
1 Tablespoon Jelly
1 Tablespoon Peanut Butter

Toast bread if desired. Then spread the jelly on one slice of bread, and spread the peanut butter on the remaining slice of bread. Put the two slices together to complete the sandwich. Cut across corner to corner to complete the perfect peanut butter and jelly.
